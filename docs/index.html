Trust is the product

Built to protect what matters.

Reclaim scans read-only, uses supported removal methods, and stops cold at anything macOS protects. Every finding lands in one of five clearly-labeled tiers.

Green — Regenerable

Caches, previews, staging. One-click, after checking the app is closed.

Blue — Reversible

Re-downloadable data and app leftovers. Quarantined with an undo window.

Yellow — History

Chat history, sessions, logs. Explained, then only removed if you choose.

Orange — Personal

Photos, messages, cloud-synced files. Never auto-deleted. Ever.

Red — Protected

System data and sealed assets. Reclaim reports them and leaves them alone.
